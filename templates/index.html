About Us

Goodwill Events Organization is a charity organization that produces entertainment, such as concerts, stand-up comedy programs, magic shows, pet shows, charity auctions, and others for charitable purposes.

Funds raised for each event go to a specific beneficiary for either prevention or relief of poverty, advancement of education, promotion of health, saving of lives, or others.

The organization is open to the public for membership through registration via our website. And only members are given the privilege to attend events and become patrons.
